Skalitude Retreat is a 160 acres nestled in the foothills of the North Cascade Mountains. It ‘is set up as a model of sustainable living, the name ‘Skalitude’ comes from a Salish word that means ‘to be in harmony with Nature’
We ask that while you are here, you respect the grounds and the nature in and around them.
While you won’t have to me hauling your own garbage home, we recommend that you keep a mindset geared toward leaving no trace; Pack it in/Pack it out.
